What is ModernCompass?
ModernCompass is a reimagining of the classic political compass with the goal of reflecting the shifts in which societal values generally describe an individual's political leaning.

Your questions / results suck!
We are not political scientists or politicians. We are politically engaged individuals with an interest in political compasses. Our worldviews are informed predominantly by western society. Others may disagree with our views on society, the questions may have underlying bias, the scope of outcomes may be too narrow... The problems with this test are potentially infinite. We do not deny this and certainly acknowledge that our interpretations are not the end-all-be-all of political theory. We can however assure you that we tried our best to make this test the best it can be. If you want to suggest changes or have constructive criticism on how we can improve the test, you're welcome to open an issue in our <a href="https://github.com/ModernCompass/ModernCompass.github.io/issues">GitHub repo</a>
