ModernCompass

What is ModernCompass?

ModernCompass is a reimagining of the classic political compass with the goal of reflecting the shifts in which societal values generally describe an individual's political leaning. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

There are questions in the test.

Your questions / results suck!

We are not political scientists or politicians. We are politically engaged individuals with an interest in political compasses. Our worldviews are informed predominantly by western society. Others may disagree with our views on society, the questions may have underlying bias, the scope of outcomes may be too narrow... The possible problems with this test are potentially infinite. We do not deny this and acknowledge that our interpretations are very probably flawed in many ways. But we can promise you that we tried our best to make this test as good as it can be.

If you'd like to suggest changes or have constructive criticism on how we can improve the test, you're welcome to open an issue in our GitHub repo.
